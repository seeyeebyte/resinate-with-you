# Artist Application And Review

这份文档可以直接变成网站上的 Apply to Join 页面和后台审核标准。

## 1. 申请页标题

英文：

> Apply to Join Resinate With You

副标题：

> We are building a creator discovery and co-creation platform for independent resin artists. Customers discover your work here, then visit your own shop, Instagram, or website to purchase directly or start a custom request.

中文解释：

> 我们正在建立一个精选滴胶作者目录。消费者在这里发现你的作品，然后跳转到你自己的店铺、Instagram 或网站购买。

### Authorization

必选 checkbox：

> I confirm that I own or have permission to use the product photos and information submitted here. I authorize Resinate With You to display my submitted photos, brand name, product details, and contact or shop links on the platform for promotional and directory purposes.

中文解释：

> 我确认我拥有或有权使用提交的图片和商品信息，并授权 Resinate With You 在平台上展示我的图片、品牌名、商品信息和店铺链接，用于推广和目录展示。

必选 checkbox：

中文解释：

> 我理解 Resinate With You 不处理付款或订单，用户会跳转到我的外部店铺、网站或社交账号购买或联系我。

## 3. 提交成功页面

英文：

> Thanks for applying. Your application has been received and will be reviewed before your artist profile is published.

中文解释：

> 感谢申请。你的资料已提交，我们审核后才会发布作者主页。

同一个 email 只能提交一条申请。申请表会在用户输入邮箱后自动检查是否已有申请；如果已有申请，提示用户前往申请状态页面查看，不再创建新的申请。

## 4. 审核标准

建议用 5 个维度审核：

1. 图片质量
   - 图片清楚
   - 能看出作品细节
   - 不全是低清截图

2. 作品相关性
   - 作品确实是滴胶成品
   - 商品适合消费者浏览或购买

3. 作者可信度
   - 有 Instagram、网站、Etsy 或其他可验证链接
   - 账号看起来是真实作者

4. 商品完整度
   - 有作品描述
   - 有购买或联系路径
   - 有基本价格范围

5. 平台匹配度
   - 风格适合平台
   - 不涉及明显侵权、仿牌或危险内容

## 5. 审核结果模板

### Approved

Subject:

> Your Resinate With You artist application was approved

Message:

> Hi {{name}},
>
> Thank you for applying to Resinate With You. Your artist application has been approved.
>
> You can now create your artist profile and submit up to 15 products for review. Once your products are approved, they will appear in the directory and link directly to your own shop or social page.
>
> Welcome to the early directory.

### Needs Info

Subject:

> We need a little more information for your Resinate With You application

Message:

> Hi {{name}},
>
> Thank you for applying to Resinate With You. We would love to review your application, but we need a little more information first.
>
> Please send:
>
> - Clearer product photos
> - A working shop, website, or Instagram link
> - A short description of your resin style
>
> Once received, we will continue the review.

### Rejected

Subject:

> Update on your Resinate With You application

Message:

> Hi {{name}},
>
> Thank you for applying to Resinate With You. After review, we are not able to approve your application at this stage.
>
> This may be because the submitted work does not match our current directory focus, the product information was incomplete, or we could not verify the submitted links.
>
> You are welcome to apply again in the future with updated information.

## 6. 作者商品提交规则

每个商品必须包含：

```text
Product Title
Product Category
Short Description
Price Text
External Purchase or Contact URL
1-8 Images
```

商品图片建议：

```text
主图清楚
不要有太多文字
不要使用别人的图片
尽量使用自然光或干净背景
```

## 7. 商品拒绝或需修改原因

常见原因：

```text
图片太模糊
商品描述太少
没有外部链接
外部链接无法打开
疑似侵权
商品不是滴胶相关
图片或内容不适合平台
```
