## 7. Instagram 邀请私信

### 短版

```text
Hi {{name}}, I love your resin work.

I’m building Resinate With You, a creator discovery platform where customers can find handmade resin pieces and connect directly with each artist’s own shop or Instagram.

Early artist listings are free during launch. Would you be interested in being featured?
```

### 稍正式版

```text
Hi {{name}},

I’m working on Resinate With You, a discovery and co-creation platform for independent resin artists.

The idea is simple: customers browse resin pieces and artist profiles on our site, then purchase directly from each artist’s own Shopify, Etsy, website, or Instagram. We do not process orders or take over your shop.

We’re inviting a small group of early artists for free launch listings. I really like your work and would love to include your studio if you’re interested.
```

### 针对已经有店铺的作者

```text
Hi {{name}},

I saw your resin pieces and your shop looks like a great fit for a new project I’m building.

Resinate With You is a discovery platform for resin artists. Your profile and selected products would be shown on the platform, but all purchase and contact buttons would link directly to your own shop or social page.

No platform checkout, no order handling, no change to how you already sell.

Would you like me to send the early artist application link?
```

## 8. Follow-up 私信

```text
Hi {{name}}, just wanted to follow up in case my last message got buried.

I’m inviting early resin artists to join Resinate With You for free during launch. It’s a simple directory profile with product links back to your own shop or Instagram.

No pressure, but I’d be happy to send more details if you’re interested.
```

## 9. 给作者说明平台规则

```text
Each approved artist can upload up to 15 products.
Products are reviewed before publishing to keep the directory curated.
Every product links directly to the artist’s own shop, website, or social page.
Resinate With You does not process payments, orders, shipping, or refunds.
```
